Shelterwood AI is a Canadian holding company building a portfolio of AI-native businesses.

## Our Approach

We believe artificial intelligence will transform every industry — but the biggest opportunities are in sectors that haven't yet been touched. We look for industries where the technology is outdated, the competition is slow, and the people deserve better tools.

Each business in our portfolio is built AI-native from the ground up. No bolt-on AI features. No legacy code. Just modern systems designed to solve real problems.

## Why "Shelterwood"?

In forestry, a shelterwood cut is a method where mature trees are left standing to protect and nurture the next generation of growth. The old canopy shelters the new seedlings until they're strong enough to thrive on their own.

That's what we do — use experience, domain knowledge, and patient capital to nurture new businesses until they can stand on their own.
